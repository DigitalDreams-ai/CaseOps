## Salesforce Problem

### Confirmed Facts

- The org uses **SMS-Magic** (integrated with Vonage) for outbound/inbound SMS communications.

- The notification email is being routed to **Stephanie Silva** instead of the scheduling coordinator (Jemimah) who sent the original SMS.

- **Robert** assisted with the original SMS-Magic configuration and Flow setup and is a known resource.
- This is classified as **low priority** by the support manager.

### Hypotheses

1. **Phone number–to–user mapping misconfiguration in SMS-Magic:** SMS-Magic maps inbound reply notifications to a specific Salesforce user based on phone number ownership. The sender phone number 17866404062 may be mapped to Stephanie Silva's user record in SMS-Magic's configuration (e.g., in SMS-Magic's "Sending Numbers" or "Number Assignment" settings), causing all replies to that number to notify her regardless of who actually sent the message.
2. **SMS-Magic notification setting pointing to a static user/email:** The SMS-Magic notification template or alert configuration may hardcode or default to Stephanie Silva's email (or a role/queue she owns) rather than dynamically resolving the record owner or conversation initiator.
3. **Salesforce Flow or Workflow Rule with hardcoded email address:** A Flow or workflow rule that fires on inbound SMS (new SMS History record creation) may have a hardcoded "Send Email" action pointing to Steph Silva, rather than a dynamic field reference to the record owner or assigned user.
4. **Ownership of SMS History records defaulting to Steph Silva:** If inbound SMS History records are being created with Steph Silva as the Owner (e.g., due to a default assignment rule, queue, or lead/contact ownership), SMS-Magic's notification logic may follow record ownership.
5. **Integration/system user (vcartaya) notification chain routes to Steph Silva:** The Vonage Integration user (`vcartaya`) may have Steph Silva configured as a delegate or notification recipient in their user settings or in the SMS-Magic app configuration.

### Likely Affected Metadata

- **SMS-Magic App Configuration** — "Sending Numbers" or "Number Assignment" settings within SMS-Magic (in-app configuration, not standard Salesforce metadata) — controls which user/email is associated with a given phone number for notification routing.
- **SMS-Magic Notification Templates / Alert Settings** — in-app settings that define who receives inbound reply emails and how the recipient is resolved (static vs. dynamic).
