### Confirmed Facts

- The affected Salesforce Account record is 001Rh00000GWgxNIAT.

- Only one patient/record is reported as affected at this time.
- The reporter (Steph Silva) states their own work is not affected — this is a patient-facing issue.

### Hypotheses

1. **Expired or malformed consent link:** The tokenized URL embedded in the SMS has expired or was generated incorrectly, causing a server-side failure when the patient attempts to load the page (most likely hypothesis given the "network error" symptom).
2. **Consent form hosting endpoint is unavailable:** The server or Experience Cloud site hosting the consent form is down, misconfigured, or has a broken endpoint, returning a network/connectivity error to the patient's browser.
3. **Salesforce Experience Cloud / Community site issue:** If the consent form is served via a Salesforce Experience Cloud site, the site may be inactive, the guest user profile may lack access, or a page/component may be broken.
4. **Third-party e-consent platform outage or misconfiguration:** If consent forms are hosted on a third-party platform integrated with Salesforce (e.g., DocuSign, HelloSign, a custom portal), that platform may be experiencing an outage or the integration link is broken.
5. **Vonage SMS link truncation or encoding issue:** The link in the SMS message may have been truncated or incorrectly encoded by Vonage, producing a non-functional URL.
6. **Patient device/network issue:** Less likely given the specificity of the error, but the patient's mobile browser or network could be blocking the URL (e.g., corporate proxy, firewall, browser incompatibility).

### Likely Affected Metadata

- **Salesforce Object:** Account (Account ID: 001Rh00000GWgxNIAT) — likely has related consent records or a consent status field
- **Salesforce Experience Cloud Site** (if applicable): The site hosting the consent form page — Site settings, Guest User Profile, page access
- **Salesforce Flow or Process:** Any flow responsible for generating the consent form link and triggering the Vonage SMS send
- **Vonage SMS Integration:** The messaging integration component/app (custom or AppExchange) that sends consent links via SMS — Sender ID: 17866404062
- **Custom Object or Standard Object for Consents:** Likely a custom object (e.g., `Consent__c`, `Patient_Consent__c`) or Health Cloud Consent records linked to the Account
- **Named Credential or Remote Site Setting:** If the consent form link points to an external endpoint, the Remote Site Setting or Named Credential may be relevant
- **Salesforce Health Cloud:** If 10X Health uses Health Cloud, consent management may use Health Cloud's built-in consent objects
