## Root Cause

External portal service (TexXAssignFormsAPI, https://apipatientportal-geajctewccbagmbk.z01.azurefd.net) returns "network error" when patient loads the Health History & Lifestyle Questionnaire form. 

**Key Finding:** The same Patient_Agreement configuration (Patient_Agreement_Configuration__c a5URh0000002JbGMAU) was successfully sent in the "Informed Consent_2026" form (a5VQl000001iRC2MAM), which **was approved and signed** on 2026-05-13T19:46:52Z. However, the "Health History & Lifestyle Questionnaire_2026" form (a5VQl000001iRC1MAM) **failed after 3 send attempts** and remains in "Sent" status.

This indicates:
1. NOT a global TexXAssignFormsAPI outage (Informed Consent worked)
2. NOT a patient permission issue (same patient, same config source)
3. **LIKELY: Form-specific issue OR patient data state blocking the Questionnaire form specifically**

The Autolaunch_Send_Patient_Agreements_to_Patient flow creates Patient_Agreement records and marks them with `Dispatch_Platform__c = 'MyWellnessPortal'`, but the flow itself does not make the HTTP call. The actual form URL generation and HTTP send is handled by the external TexXAssignFormsAPI integration (via Named Credential), not visible in the codebase.
