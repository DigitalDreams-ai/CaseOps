Salesforce currently has no validation rule or code-level guard to prevent values longer than 125 characters from being entered or submitted, so the error only surfaces at the moment of Wellvi submission.

The specific error on your record is:
> `VALIDATION_ERROR: RxClaim.Instructions- must be 125 characters or fewer`

3. If the value is still over 125 characters including spaces, reduce it to 125 or fewer and save.
4. Resubmit the pharmacy order.

If you have already done this and the order is still failing, it is possible that our integration is appending additional text to your instructions value before sending it to Wellvi — in that case, please let me know and we will escalate to Engineering to inspect that behavior directly.
