## Root Cause

The Operations team had no way to distinguish between appointments that were truly self-scheduled via Calendly versus those that required manual scheduler intervention on a Consultation Opportunity in Salesforce. No "Scheduled by" field existed on the Opportunity object to capture this attribution. The request is a net-new picklist field addition — not a bug, but a missing feature/configuration gap.
