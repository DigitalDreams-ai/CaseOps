# Internal Notes

**Reference:** See `outputs/investigations/HEAL-33054.md` for full problem analysis, Salesforce configuration, and similar items analysis.

## Root Cause

This was a feature/change request, not a break-fix. The reporter (Ashlee Edwards, Wellness Department) requested a new checkbox field called "Cardone Ventures Employee" on the Account (Patient) record type, with the value flowing automatically into all Opportunity record types for reporting purposes. No existing field or automation existed for this purpose — this was a net-new metadata build.

## Solution Or Escalation

Solved in Sandbox by Sean Bingham (Salesforce Admin). No engineering escalation required.

**Implementation summary:**
- Created `Cardone_Ventures_Employee__c` (Checkbox, default: false) on the Account object.
- Created `Cardone_Ventures_Employee__c` (Formula Checkbox, formula: `Account.Cardone_Ventures_Employee__c`) on the Opportunity object so the value is always derived from the master Account record — no Opportunity-level backfill needed once Accounts are updated.
- Applied FLS across relevant Permission Sets:
  - Admin_Team: Read-only on both Account and Opportunity fields.
  - Medical_Coordinator: Read-only on both.
  - Nursing_Staff: Read-only on both.
  - Operations_Manager: Read+Edit on Account; Read-only on Opportunity (formula, non-editable by nature).
  - Operations_Team: Read-only on both.
- Added the Account field to 6 Account/PersonAccount page layouts (Account Summary section, after `Do_Not_Sync_to_Biocanic__c`).
- Added the Opportunity formula field (Read-only) to all 31 in-use Opportunity page layouts, placed after `AccountId` in the "Opportunity Information" section.
- PRs #9062 and #9065 raised; Gearset deployment initiated (deploymentId: `2ceeeedb-b80b-435f-9d4f-ada3979c553b`).

**Retroactive data backfill:** Existing Cardone Ventures employee Accounts must be manually identified and the checkbox ticked at the Account level. Opportunity records will reflect the correct value automatically once the Account is updated. Ivelisse Santos confirmed (2026-05-12) that the team will go back and manually select CV Employee on relevant accounts — no automated backfill tooling is needed.

**Open question answered:** Sean asked how to identify which Accounts are CV employee Accounts. Ivelisse Santos confirmed there is no existing flag and the team will manually identify and mark them.
