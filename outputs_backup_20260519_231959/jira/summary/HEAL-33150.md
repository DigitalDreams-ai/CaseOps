- Portal summary (Summary): 
- Portal description (Description): The Cx Case Record type does not receive or attach responses when messaging internally/externally. Once the messages continue the thread which attaches the messages to cases is not included. We need to make sure the thread is included for all responses can be seen in the email chain on cases. Below I have examples, if more information or explanation is needed please let me know. Example 1: Created a case in Salesforce and sent an email through the case to Tyler. The email included the case thread number as expected. Tyler did receive the email successfully, but it was delivered to her via Outlook. When Tyler responded to that email, her reply went through Outlook instead of syncing back into Salesforce. Because of this, her response did not log under the case activity in Salesforce as it normally should. As a result, the conversation thread is incomplete within the case. Only the initial email I sent to Tyler appears. The next email that appears in Salesforce is a separate message I sent to the client confirming the refund approval. This means the full back-and-forth with Tyler is not reflected in Salesforce, and the case does not show the full communication history as a proper paper trail.
