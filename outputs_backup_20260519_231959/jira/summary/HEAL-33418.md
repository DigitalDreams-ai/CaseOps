### 2026-05-11T12:09:50.817-0400 - Sean Bingham (internal/unknown)

The issue appears to be in flow Autolaunch_Send_Patient_Agreements_to_Patient. Current behavior: The flow checks for existing Informed Consent Patient Agreement records. Its matching logic is too broad. It counts expired/incomplete Informed Consent records as valid matches. Because of that, the flow does not create/send a new Informed Consent and attempts to update the existing Patient Agreement record. In this case, the flow attempted to update the Patient Agreement ‘a5VRh000004BUAPMA4’ on the Account '0010b00002dC2y2AAC' A possible fix: Update the flow logic so expired or incomplete Informed Consent records are not treated as valid existing consent matches. Thank you, Sean Bingham Salesforce Administrator 10X Health Help Desk Support
