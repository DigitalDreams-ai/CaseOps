# HEAL-32826 Jira Response

## Internal Notes

**Root Cause:** Sync flow from Shopify to Salesforce failed during 3/18–3/27/2026 when populating "Informed Consent 2026" on Account records for MGT/GHT orders. A fix deployed 3/27 restored consent population for new orders, but ~300+ affected accounts created during the failure window remain without consent data.

**Why Escalated:** Engineering investigation required to:
1. Identify what broke the sync during 3/18–3/27 and confirm 3/27 fix is permanent (not a temporary workaround)
2. Clarify whether consent removal post-Chrono sync is a design intent (missing feature) or broken workflow
3. Validate "Assign patient agreements" bulk action for safe backfill of ~300+ affected accounts
4. Coordinate with eComm (Adam Botma spike ticket) to confirm Shopify sent consent data during failure window

**Support Impact:** Hundreds of patients may lack required consent records; backfill strategy and permanent fix required before closure.
