# Problem Hypothesis — HEAL-32826

## Confirmed Facts

From Jira HEAL-32826 and Issue Understanding:
- Hundreds of PAM cases (3/18–3/27/2026) for Shopify MGT/GHT orders lack "Informed Consent 2026" on associated SF accounts.
- A fix was deployed on 3/27/2026 that restored forward-looking assignment (post-3/27 orders show consent correctly assigned).
- Consent assignment mechanism likely involves an SF automation (flow or trigger) that fires on account/opportunity creation from Shopify order sync.
- The automation failed silently during the 3/18–3/27 window but was restored by the 3/27 fix.
- SF→Chrono sync behavior post-backfill is unknown; SF removal step has not been observed to function historically.

## Root Cause Hypothesis

**One sentence:** SF automation (flow or trigger on Account/Opportunity object) responsible for assigning Informed Consent 2026 on Shopify MGT/GHT order sync failed during 3/18–3/27/2026 due to unknown cause; 3/27 fix deployed to restore it for forward-looking orders, but affected records in the window remain unbackfilled.

## Smallest Viable Fix

1. Identify the specific SF automation component (flow name or trigger name) changed in the 3/27 deployment.
2. Confirm what was broken and what the fix changed.
3. Confirm whether "Assign patient agreements" action in SF correctly and safely backfills Informed Consent 2026 to affected accounts without duplication or incorrect downstream state.
4. If confirmed safe: batch-execute "Assign patient agreements" on all affected accounts from the 3/18–3/27 window in Production.
5. Verify SF→Chrono sync behavior post-backfill (no duplicates, correct downstream records, consent removal from SF if expected).
6. If systemic defect confirmed: open ADO bug ticket.

## Sandbox Validation Plan

Observe Informed Consent 2026 is created correctly without duplication. Monitor sync pipeline to Chrono (if testable in Sandbox).

**Expected outcome:** Informed Consent 2026 is assigned; no duplicate records created; SF→Chrono sync functions without error.

**Success criteria:** Cloned account shows Informed Consent 2026 post-action; no duplicate consent records; no downstream Chrono errors.

## Rollback Plan

If Sandbox testing reveals duplicates or incorrect state:
1. Do not proceed to Production backfill.
2. Delete incorrectly assigned records in Sandbox.
3. Escalate root cause to Engineering (Sean Bingham).
4. Await Engineering analysis of why "Assign patient agreements" created duplicates.
