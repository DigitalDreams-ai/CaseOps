## Status

In Progress — SMS-Magic email notification is firing to the wrong Salesforce user (Stephanie Silva) when a patient replies to a text originally sent by a scheduling coordinator (Jemimah).

## Root Cause

SMS-Magic is sending the inbound message notification email to Stephanie Silva instead of the agent who sent the original outbound SMS. The notification email originates from `vcartaya@10xhealthsystem.com` (Vonage Integration user) and references SMS History ID `aADQl000001UJFx`. The likely cause is that the SMS-Magic notification flow or workflow rule resolves the recipient of the inbound reply notification from a field that is incorrectly populated — either the SMS record's owner, a lookup to the wrong user, or a shared/misconfigured Vonage number-to-user mapping. Robert was involved in the original flow/configuration and is a key resource. A prior related issue (HEAL-33537) involved Stephanie's own number being misconfigured; Idman reportedly fixed that one, suggesting the fix may have partially corrected one record but left a systemic misconfiguration in place that now surfaces for Jemimah's records.

## Decision

**Escalate to Engineering**

Confidence: High. This is a Salesforce SMS-Magic flow/automation misconfiguration requiring investigation of the SMS record ownership, the notification flow logic, and potentially the Vonage number-to-user mapping table. Support cannot safely modify SMS-Magic flow configuration or Salesforce automation rules without engineering review. Robert's prior involvement makes Engineering the correct handler.
