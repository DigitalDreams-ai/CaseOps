## Status

Escalated to Engineering (eComm/Shopify integration team). Shopify-to-Salesforce order sync failed to invoke consent assignment flow for 9-day window (3/18–3/27). 3/27 fix restored mechanism for forward-looking orders; backfill of affected accounts (several hundred) requires Engineering confirmation and data remediation approach.

## Root Cause

The Shopify-to-SF order sync integration (`Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements` flow invocation) failed during 3/18–3/27 to either:
- Advance Opportunities to "Waiting For Approval"/"Approved" stage, OR
- Populate `Transaction_Source__c = "Store"` on Shopify-sourced Opportunities

Without these trigger conditions met, the Patient Agreement flow never fired, leaving affected accounts without Informed Consent 2026 records. The flow itself (`Autolaunch_Send_Patient_Agreements_to_Patient`) is syntactically correct and active; the entry point from Shopify sync was the failure.

## Decision

**Escalate to Engineering**

High confidence: Investigation confirms the Patient Agreement automation is working correctly in Production (API 62.0, active, no syntax errors). Post-3/27 behavior (new orders correctly assigned) proves the flow works. The failure point is the Shopify-to-SF integration layer that invokes this flow. Only eComm team (Adam Botma + Shopify integration SME) can identify what broke during 3/18–3/27 and confirm backfill approach.
