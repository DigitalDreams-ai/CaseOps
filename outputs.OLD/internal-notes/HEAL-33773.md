## Root Cause

Flow [Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Shopify_Automations](sf://300Rh000002XrSdIAK) submits Opportunities to approval unconditionally without checking Amount_Paid__c; approval process automatically sets Status to "Waiting for Approval" on submission. Flow also lacks idempotency protection, allowing re-syncs to revert approved records.

## Decision

**Support-Resolvable** — High confidence.

**Evidence:** Production metadata inspection confirmed the flow routing logic skips payment validation before approval submission (lines 917-1041, 603-631). Approval process mechanism automatically sets Status field on submission. Test records confirm Amount_Paid__c is NULL or unevaluated before routing. Sandbox test confirmed both conditions (payment check + idempotency) now working.
