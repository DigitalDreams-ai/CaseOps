# Internal Notes — HEAL-33054

**Reference:** See `outputs/investigations/HEAL-33054.md` for full problem diagnosis, Salesforce configuration, and similar items analysis.

## Status

Implementation complete in Sandbox; pending Production deployment and retroactive Account-level data backfill by business team.

## Root Cause

No defect — this is a net-new feature request. The "Cardone Ventures Employee" checkbox did not exist on Account or Opportunity. The business needed a way to flag CV employees at the Account level and surface that flag across all Opportunity record types for reporting.

## Decision

**Support-Resolvable**

Confidence: High. Sean Bingham built and documented the complete solution: Account checkbox (`Cardone_Ventures_Employee__c`), Opportunity formula field referencing `Account.Cardone_Ventures_Employee__c`, FLS across all relevant permission sets, and field placement on all applicable Account and Opportunity page layouts (31 Opportunity layouts, 6 Account/PersonAccount layouts). PRs #9062 and #9065 have been raised. No code or automation complexity requiring Engineering involvement.

## Risks

- Retroactive backfill is entirely manual — no automation exists to identify CV employee Accounts; risk of missed records if business does not have a clean list.
- Formula field on Opportunity is read-only by design; if business later needs to override at Opportunity level independently of Account, a schema change will be required.
- No validation rule prevents the Account checkbox from being unchecked after Opportunities are created — downstream reporting could silently change if Account is updated.
