- System description: We have often opened WC or PI cases for clients that have active pending litigation on a PI case. This causes issues when the PI case is in litigation, possibly nearing a settlement, and we open a new accident case with overlapping injuries and never consult the client about the ramifications of opening a new case as it pertains to their existing lawsuit. Opening these claims could lead to new lines of questioning in a case with a scheduled mediation, possibly prolonging a lawsuit another 2-3 years. Additionally, it could create an event where the handling personal injury lawyer was never advised the client had a new accident, the defense council could file motions or speak about this new accident in front of a judge and our lawyer could be working under the assumption there are no new accidents. I am requesting a requirement on new intakes with existing PI cases to please notify the handling attorney and/or Seth about the new accident before opening the case and setting the client up for treatment. These events need to be communicated to the whole team working on the case, including the WC teams. It should be nearly impossible for an intake staff to open a case without reviewing it with the handling PI attorney .
