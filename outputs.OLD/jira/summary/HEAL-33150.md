### 2026-05-21T18:04:00.767-0400 - Sean Bingham (public)

Hey Noah, With the current standard Salesforce email composer configuration, we cannot limit the sender dropdown to only two choices. It is either a free-for-all (currently in use), or we lock the sender to one email address. The other option would be to build a custom email component, which would give us greater control over exactly which sender addresses are available. Salesforce adds email threading identifiers to outbound Case emails so Email-to-Case can match replies back to the same Case. Threading (using a code) primarily uses email headers, but a visible code may also appear in the email body as a fallback. I tested sending and replying through Salesforce with and without the visible body code, and the reply still attached correctly when the reply was routed back into Salesforce only when using a Salesforce-routed Email-to-Case address. If the reply only goes to a user mailbox or shared Outlook mailbox and does not route into Salesforce, Salesforce cannot attach it to the Case. Thank you, Sean Bingham Salesforce Administrator 10X Health Help Desk Support
