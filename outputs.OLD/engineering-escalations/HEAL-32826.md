## Root Cause

**Confirmed (via Production investigation):** Shopify-to-SF order sync integration failed to invoke the consent assignment flow during 3/18–3/27. The Patient Agreement flows themselves (`Autolaunch_Send_Patient_Agreements_to_Patient` and `Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements`) exist and are syntactically correct in Production (API 62.0, active, no errors). The failure point is the Shopify sync invocation layer: either Opportunities were not advancing to "Waiting For Approval"/"Approved" stage, or the `Transaction_Source__c = "Store"` field was not being populated during the sync. Without these trigger conditions, the flows never fired.

The 3/27 fix restored forward-looking assignment by either re-enabling Opportunity stage advancement or correcting the Transaction_Source population in the Shopify sync. Post-3/27 Shopify orders correctly receive Informed Consent 2026.

## Problem Location (Step 6)

**Problem Type:** Integration (Shopify-to-SF sync invocation)

**Artifact:** `Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements` flow (the entry point for Shopify order sync). Requires Opportunity to reach "Waiting For Approval" OR "Approved" stage AND Transaction_Source = "Store" to invoke downstream consent flows.

**Location:** Production > Setup > Flows > `Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements`; linked to `flows/Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements.flow-meta.xml` in repo. Also Shopify app integration that syncs orders and must populate Transaction_Source and advance stage on Opportunities.

**Failure Point:** During 3/18–3/27, the Shopify→SF sync failed to either:
  1. Advance Opportunities to the required stage ("Waiting For Approval" or "Approved"), OR
  2. Populate `Transaction_Source__c = "Store"` on Shopify-sourced Opportunities

Without both conditions met, the `Record_Trigger_Opportunity_Send_Patient_Agreements` flow never fired, and the downstream `Autolaunch_Send_Patient_Agreements_to_Patient` subflow (which creates the Patient_Agreement__c records) was never invoked. This left hundreds of accounts without Informed Consent 2026 records.
