# Jira Message — HEAL-33773

**CUSTOMER-FACING — POSTED TO JIRA**

Hi Sarah,

I investigated and resolved this issue in Sandbox.

**Root cause:**

Your Shopify POS transactions were being routed to "Waiting for Approval" without checking whether payment was collected. When a transaction came through a re-sync, it could revert a previously approved record back to "Waiting for Approval" because the automation lacked protection against re-submission.

**What was changed:**

Updated the Shopify automation to:
1. Verify that payment was received before routing transactions to approval. Transactions with zero payment now skip the approval step.
2. Add a check to prevent previously approved transactions from being re-submitted on subsequent syncs.

**Production vs Sandbox:**

The fix was validated in Sandbox only. Production has not been updated by this work. Your team will need to promote these changes to Production via Gearset.
