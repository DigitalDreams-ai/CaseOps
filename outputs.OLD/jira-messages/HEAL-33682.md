Thank you for bringing this to our attention. I've completed an investigation into the orphan booklet orders you reported.

## What We Found

The three affected Lab Orders were originally submitted to Novogenia with a request to include the "Report Print & Shipment Fee" (#VRSF) product code. However, the corresponding Salesforce order records were not created to track these shipments. This mismatch explains why Novogenia has records of the printed reports being produced, but our system doesn't show the associated orders.

The Lab Orders in question were submitted in 2024 and earlier, so the detailed submission logs are no longer available in our standard support access window.

## What Happens Next

Because we need log-level access to trace why the Salesforce order records weren't created when the Novogenia requests were made, I'm escalating this to our Engineering team. They have access to deeper system logs and can investigate the root cause of the missing records.

We'll follow up with you once they've completed their review.
