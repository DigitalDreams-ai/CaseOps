# HEAL-33889 — Resolution: ShipStation OAuth Reauthorization Required

Hi Michael,

Thanks for providing the error screenshots and order details. I've completed a full investigation of the order sync issue affecting LaCore integration.

## What I Found

Orders created in Salesforce on 2026-05-28 onwards are not syncing to LaCore. The error message "Authorization failed for SalesforceCore store SellerID:4177129 and StoreID:793381" points to the ShipStation side.

## Root Cause

The OAuth token that connects your Salesforce store to ShipStation has expired or been revoked on the ShipStation platform. This happened between the last successful sync at 8:48 PM on 2026-05-28 and when the error started appearing.

The good news: Salesforce itself is working correctly. The order sync triggers and handlers are active and ready. The problem is purely the expired credential between Salesforce and ShipStation.

## What Happens Next

To fix this, you need to reauthorize the ShipStation connection from your ShipStation admin console:

1. Log in to ShipStation admin
2. Find your Salesforce store connector settings (SellerID:4177129, StoreID:793381)
3. Look for a "Reconnect" or "Reauthorize" button and click it
4. Complete the OAuth flow to grant Salesforce permissions again
5. Once reauth is complete, create a test order in Salesforce to verify the sync works

I tested the Salesforce side in sandbox and confirmed all integration components are active and functioning. No code changes or Salesforce configuration updates are needed.

## Next Steps

Once you've completed the ShipStation reauthorization, new orders should sync automatically again. If the sync continues to fail after reauth, let me know and I can escalate to ShipStation support or dig deeper on the Salesforce side.
