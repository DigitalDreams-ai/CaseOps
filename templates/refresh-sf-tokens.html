🔄 Refresh Salesforce Access Tokens

Salesforce access tokens expire after 8 hours. Follow these steps to refresh them.

Step 1: Get Current Tokens (from your local machine)

1

Open terminal on your authenticated local machine and run:

sf org auth show-access-token -o 10xhealth --no-prompt

Copy the entire token value

2

Then get the sandbox token:

sf org auth show-access-token -o 10xhealth-sean --no-prompt

Copy the entire token value

⏱️ Token Expiry Timeline

• Refresh timestamp set: Pipeline can run freely for 6 hours
• After 6 hours: Warning shown at startup, but pipeline still works
• After 8 hours: Tokens expire, pipeline fails to execute SOQL queries
Refresh tokens before they expire!
