🔄 Refresh Salesforce Access Tokens

Salesforce access tokens expire after 8 hours. Follow these steps to refresh them.

Step 1: Get Current Tokens (from your local machine)

1

Open terminal on your authenticated local machine and run:

sf org auth show-access-token -o 10xhealth --no-prompt

Copy the entire token value

2

Then get the sandbox token:

sf org auth show-access-token -o 10xhealth-sean --no-prompt

Copy the entire token value

Step 2: Paste Tokens Below

Access Tokens (Required)

Refresh Tokens (Optional but Recommended for Auto-Refresh)

If you have refresh tokens, paste them below to enable automatic token refresh every 4 hours. Without refresh tokens, you'll need to manually refresh every 8 hours.

⏱️ Token Expiry & Auto-Refresh Timeline

Without Refresh Tokens (Manual Refresh):
• 0–4 hours: Pipeline works freely
• 4–8 hours: Warning at startup, pipeline may have reduced Salesforce access
• After 8 hours: Tokens expire, Salesforce queries fail

With Refresh Tokens (Auto-Refresh):
• Every 4 hours: Tokens automatically refresh in the background
• No manual intervention needed — pipeline continues working indefinitely
Recommendation: Provide refresh tokens for continuous operation.
