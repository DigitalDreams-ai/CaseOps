# Jira Message Draft

<!--
Customer-facing only. Output should be conversational, friendly, direct, human,
and non-technical. Do not include internal diagnosis notes, metadata dumps,
file paths, pipeline details, or private IDs unless the reporter specifically
asked for technical detail.
-->

Hi [Name],

[Plain-language summary of what was happening. Keep this to one or two short sentences.]

[Plain-language explanation of the cause. Explain the user-facing reason, not the full Salesforce implementation.]

[Plain-language explanation of the solution or next step. Say whether the fix was confirmed in Sandbox, whether Production still needs a deploy, or whether no metadata deploy is needed.]

[Optional: Ask for confirmation, mention timing, or offer a specific next step.]

[Optional: Thank them for specific useful detail they provided, such as screenshots, record examples, or reproduction steps.]

## Voice checklist

- No "we", "we've", "we're", "us", or "let us".
- No em dashes.
- No corporate filler.
- No heavy Salesforce jargon unless the reporter asked for technical detail.
- Do not imply Production is fixed when only Sandbox was validated.
- Keep it short enough to paste into Jira without editing.
